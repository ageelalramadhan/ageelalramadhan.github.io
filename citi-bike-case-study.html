NYC Citi Bike Dashboard (2022)

Python • Pandas/NumPy • Data storytelling • Operational recommendations

Project Overview

This project analyzes Citi Bike usage patterns in New York City during 2022 to support operational decision-making around bike supply, rebalancing, and seasonal demand planning. The goal was to translate trip-level data into actionable operational recommendations.

Business Problem

Citi Bike demand fluctuates by location, time of day, and season. The objective was identifying high-demand stations, understanding seasonal usage patterns, and determining how operations should adapt to reduce empty docks and unavailable bikes.

Data

The dataset included Citi Bike trip data for 2022 and corresponding weather data for seasonal comparison. Analysis was performed using Python (Pandas, NumPy) with visualizations built for an interactive Streamlit dashboard.

Process

  • Cleaned and validated trip data (dates, station names, ride durations)
  • Aggregated trips by station, day, and month
  • Merged daily trip counts with temperature data
  • Ranked top 10 highest-demand stations
  • Built dashboard views (stations, seasonality, maps, recommendations)

Key Insights

  • Demand is heavily concentrated in central Manhattan stations
  • Trips increase significantly during warmer months and decline sharply in winter
  • Peak demand aligns with predictable commuting windows
  • Winter demand averages roughly half of summer demand levels

Visual Evidence

A few key charts from the dashboard that support the insights above.

1) Seasonality drives demand

2) Temperature strongly influences ridership

3) Highest-demand stations are concentrated in Manhattan

Recommendations

  • Increase rebalancing frequency at high-demand Manhattan stations
  • Scale staffing and operations seasonally (summer vs winter)
  • Implement targeted early-morning and mid-day rebalancing cycles
  • Use spatial hotspot data to evaluate expansion corridors

Conclusion

This analysis demonstrates how time-series trends, spatial clustering, and demand concentration can guide operational efficiency in mobility systems. By aligning supply decisions with observed demand patterns, Citi Bike can improve reliability and optimize resource allocation.
