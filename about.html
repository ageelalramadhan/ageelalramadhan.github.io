About Me

Hi, I'm Ageel. I'm a Data Analyst with a background in performance marketing. I transitioned into data analytics because I enjoy investigating why performance changes, validating assumptions, and turning messy metrics into clear, decision-ready insights.

I bring a performance-driven mindset to data analysis, combining marketing context with structured analytics to deliver measurable business impact. My work focuses on growth, customer behavior, and KPI movement — translating data patterns into practical, measurable business actions.
